System info

The labor market report is one of the main monthly indicators that indicate the outcome of economic activity because it covers all the basic sectors of the state economy. It is comprehensive and announced early in the month. Many other economic indicators depend on its data. It not only provides information on the labor market but also shows the level of income and production. In short, it provides basic information for other economic indicators released during the month and plays an active role in the financial impact on markets
